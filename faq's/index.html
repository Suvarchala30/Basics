How much is the course fees?
If you wish to pay upfront, you can pay us ₹ 49,999/-. Job guarantee does not apply in case you choose upfront option though the training and placement opportunities will be the same as Pay after placement.
  • If you wish to pay after placement, you can pay us from your salary post course completion. Job with salary between ₹5 - ₹10LPA, you pay us ₹ 10,000 each month for 12 months i.e. total ₹ 1,20,000 + GST
  • Job with salary between ₹10 - ₹18LPA, you pay us ₹ 15,000 each month for 12 months i.e. total ₹ 1,80,000 + GST
  • Job with salary above ₹18LPA, you pay us ₹ 20,000 each month for 12 months i.e. total ₹ 2,40,000 + GST
